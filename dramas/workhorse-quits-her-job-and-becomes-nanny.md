# English Title: Niu Ma Quits Her Job to Become a Nanny and Tames a Boss with Anorexia 

Original name: 牛马辞职当保姆，拿捏厌食症大佬

Female actor:Peng Yao 彭瑶

Male actor: Zhao Guanyu 赵冠宇

Summary: An office workhorse finds out her boyfriend who is her boss is cheating on her so she quits her job. A higher paying nanny position is available so she applies for the job to become a rich anorexic CEO's food nanny. As she cures him and they get closer, they fall in love.
