## Icelandic waters with Icebergs

### Ingredients

- [ ] white rum
- [ ] orange juice (orangey juices work like grapefruit or peach juice or lemonade)
- [ ] peach snapps
- [ ] triple sec
- [ ] non alcoholic blue caracao
- [ ] lemon juice
- [ ] dash of angostura bitters
- [ ] ice cubes

###

- [ ] best if shaken in cocktail shaker
- [ ] pour blue caracao carefully so the drink is layered green on top
