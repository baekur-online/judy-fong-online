# Steamed Egg

## Ingredients
- [ ] 3 eggs
- [ ] slight larger volume of water than eggs
- [ ] large shrimps or dried small shrimps (hami)
- [ ] green onion for garnish
- [ ] soy sauce

## Instructions
1. Mix the eggs together
2. Add water then shrimp. Everything should be in a large flat bowl/metal tray that fits in the steamer.
3. Steam until the egg is firm but not overcooked. Drizzle a bit of soy sauce to taste along with chopped green onions. Enjoy with rice!
