# Kimchi noodle soup

## Ingredients
- [ ] veggie ramyun package
- [ ] snap peas
- [ ] silken tofu
- [ ] 1 cup of kimchi
- [ ] 5 large peeled shrimp
- [ ] 2-3 shiitake
- [ ] 1 tablespoon gochujang
- [ ] 1 small sliced potato
- [ ] a dash of fish sauce
- [ ] half a teaspoon of sugar
- [ ] 1 liter of water
- [ ] 250 grams pork shoulder sliced (optional)
- [ ] green onion (optional)

# Method

1. Mix the fish sauce, gochujang, sugar, noodle seasoning and dried veggies, and kimchi in water. 
2. Cook the potato for 45 mins. 
3. add in the sliced shiitake and tofu and snap peas. 
4. Add in the noodles and shrimp for 4 1/2 mins.
