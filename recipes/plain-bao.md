# Non sweet mantou bao

## Ingredients
- [ ] 4g yeast
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- [ ] 120-127 g milk
- [ ] 10 g granulated sugar
- [ ] 200 g all-purpose flour
- [ ] 25 g cooking oil

## Process
- [ ] warm up milk on the stovetop or in a microwave
- [ ] Combine all ingredients
- [ ] Knead for 3-4 mins
- [ ] leave it to sit in a warm place covered for 60 mins
- [ ] pre-heat steamer
- [ ] roll out the dough into thin layers with a rolling pin then roll it into buns
- [ ] place each bun on parchment paper to prevent it from sticking to the steamer
- [ ] steam for 15 mins
- [ ] Enjoy!

## 

- makes about 8
