# Tuna Salad

## Ingredients
- [ ] canned tuna, in oil or water, drained
- [ ] diced onion
- [ ] mayonnaise
- [ ] diced celery, optional
- [ ] a hard boiled egg, optional
- [ ] onion powder
- [ ] garlic powder
- [ ] salt
- [ ] mustard, optional
- [ ] relish, optional

## Instructions
1. Combine and refrigerate or enjoy right away
