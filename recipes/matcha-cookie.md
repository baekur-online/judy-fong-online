# Matcha Cookies

have not tried yet

## Ingredients
- [ ] 225 grams softened butter
- [ ] 1 1/2 brown sugar or granulated
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla
- [ ] 1 dollop of peanut butter
- [ ] 1 stick of matcha tea powder mix
- [ ] 1 tsp baking soda
- [ ] 1/2 tsp baking powder
- [ ] 2 3/4 cups flour


## process
0. preheat oven to 190 C
1. mix butter sugar egg and vanilla together matcha powder and peanut
2. then add in baking powder soda and flour
3. bake at 190 C for 8-10 mins
