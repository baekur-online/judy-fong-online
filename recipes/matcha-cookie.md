# Matcha Cookies

makes about 20 cookies, two trays

## Ingredients
- [ ] 160 grams softened salted butter
- [ ] 190 grams brown sugar or granulated or less
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 1/2 tsp vanilla
- [ ] 1 dollop of peanut butter
- [ ] 1 stick of matcha tea powder mix
- [ ] 3/4 tsp baking soda
- [ ] 1/2 tsp baking powder
- [ ] 220 grams flour


## process
0. preheat oven to 185 C
1. mix butter sugar egg and vanilla together matcha powder and peanut
2. then add in baking powder soda and flour
3. bake at 185 C for 8-10 mins
