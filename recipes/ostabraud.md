# Ostabrauð

## preparation

1. Place frozen ostbrauð in Ninja air fryer
2. Set to air fry for 17 mins at 180 C. You may want a lower temp if you want less brown bread.

3. not attempted yet Turn over half way through

## Oven preparation

1. Preheat oven to 180 C heating both top and botton, no fan. 
2. Place cheese balls on a flat surface so pie pan or baking sheet. If the baking sheat is slottes, use baking paper to keep the balls even and together or they're liable to fall through the slots.
3. Take them out 30 mins later and let cool to a reasonable temperature. They should have grown two to three times in size.
